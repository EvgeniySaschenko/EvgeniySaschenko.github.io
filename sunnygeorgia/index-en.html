Route

In this excursion you will see the beauty of mountainous Ajara, famous waterfall Makhuntseti and an old bridge built in the time of Queen Tamara (thousand years). Then we will visit a wine house, where will be having traditional lunch/dinner and Wine degustation made local grapes.

THE BEGINNING OF THE TOUR

The tour starts in the old Batumi (intersection V. Pshavela and M. Abashidze 50), if You leave nearby, you can walk straight to the gathering place. If not, we will be able to pick you up from Your location.

1st STOP

Then we will head to famous Ajarianroad towards the village of Keda. Only in 20 minutes after departure you'll be able to see an amazing view of mountain serpentine. First stop: The Makhuntseti Waterfall (height of about 30 meters). Within a 5-minute walk from the bridge there will be Bridge of Queen Tamara (dated ~11 century and 29 metres long). You can make great pictures and purchase various tradidtional Souvenirs, honey, etc.

2nd STOP (FOR ALL INCLUSIVE TRIPS)

Next, we will be heading to a private estate - Shervashidze Wine House, which located among the mountains of Adjara. Beautiful sightseeing and the hospitality of the owners will not leave you indiferrent . You will be able to taste local wines and dishes of the Georgian and Ajarian cuisine. Here you will be able to purchase wine from local grapes and famous local drink called “Chacha”.

Extra

At Shervashidze Wine House there will be a “Zip Line” 160 meters Long. ( Rope that is suspended above an incline to which a pulley and harness will be attached to you. The incline allows you to be pulled by gravity along the line.) If you want you can fly with above the vineyards for 5 GEL.

The tour ends at Europe square, in the centre of Batumi.
